Aggro

Genre: MMO/RPG

Aggro is a term used to describe the attention of enemies in a videogame. I like to think of it as a shortened word for aggression. To take the aggro means to "draw the ire" of an enemy, meaning they would target whoever took aggro. How this is accomplished varies from game to game, but generally whoever does the most damage or whoever attacked the enemy first will get the aggro. It is possible to shift aggro in some games. In games where characters are more specialized in their roles, usually the tank, or player who can withstand the most damage, will prioritize the aggro, so the players/characters with less health can contribute in their own ways without dying.
